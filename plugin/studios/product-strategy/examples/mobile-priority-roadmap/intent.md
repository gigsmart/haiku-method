# H1 2026 Mobile-Priority Roadmap

H1 2026 product roadmap with native mobile as top priority. Deprecate the web-shell wrapper in favor of a standalone native app. Launch bar is core-workflow parity with web + offline read support. Collaboration and admin features defer to H2.

## Goals

- Identify and validate the core workflows users do on mobile today (research + telemetry)
- Prioritize workstreams against engineering capacity and launch bar
- Sequence the roadmap so the native app ships in Q2 with offline-read in Q3
- Stakeholder review and sign-off from exec + eng leadership
