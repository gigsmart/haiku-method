# AI Platform vs. Portfolio Strategy

Frame and decide whether the company pursues a unified AI platform strategy or doubles down on the two revenue-concentrated flagship products. Decision memo due at the next board meeting (6 weeks out). Current revenue is 70% concentrated in two flagships; the remaining 30% is spread across six smaller products.

## Goals

- Landscape scan of platform vs. portfolio plays by direct and adjacent competitors
- Three concrete options with revenue, cost, and timeline modeling
- Clear recommendation tied to the 70/30 revenue concentration reality
- Board-ready communication deck and pre-read memo
