# Options

## Criteria Guidance

Good criteria examples:
- "Options matrix includes at least 3 distinct strategic paths with clearly differentiated value propositions"
- "Each option has a financial model with stated assumptions, investment required, and projected returns"
- "Options span a range of risk/reward profiles — not all variations of the same conservative approach"

Bad criteria examples:
- "Options are generated"
- "Models are built"
- "Alternatives are listed"

## Completion Signal

Options matrix exists with distinct strategic alternatives, each with financial model, resource requirements, and risk profile. Ideator has ensured options are genuinely differentiated and not variations of one approach. Modeler has validated financial assumptions and quantified key uncertainties.
