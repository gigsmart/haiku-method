# Mutual NDA for Data Processor

Draft a mutual NDA for a new data-processor vendor relationship. Delaware law, 3-year term from the effective date. Cover customer PII (our side) and proprietary pipeline internals (their side).

## Goals

- Mutual protection — both parties share confidential information
- Standard carveouts for publicly-available and independently-developed information
- Return-or-destroy provision at termination
- Survival clause: confidentiality obligations persist 5 years post-term
