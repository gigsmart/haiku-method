# Inception

Understand the market, user problem, and business case. Same shape as
application development inception — what are we building and why, who would
buy it, what is the competitive landscape. Hardware-specific constraints
(safety, regulatory, manufacturing feasibility) surface in the requirements
stage, not here.

## Completion Signal (RFC 2119)

Discovery document **MUST** identify target users and business case.
Competitive landscape **MUST** be assessed. Rough cost envelope **MUST** be
bounded. Units **MUST** have verifiable criteria.
