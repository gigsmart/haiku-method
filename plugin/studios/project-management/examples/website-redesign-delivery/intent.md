# Website Redesign Delivery

Deliver the website redesign by the customer summit (hard date, day 30 of the quarter). Design is approved, content is 50% drafted, engineering hasn't started — eng is the critical path. Backward-plan from the summit date with a weekly status cadence and explicit go/no-go at day 20.

## Goals

- Charter and RACI with explicit critical path (engineering)
- Backward-planned schedule with 10-day buffer before the summit
- Weekly status with risk burn-down visible to exec sponsor
- Day-20 go/no-go decision point with a documented rollback plan
